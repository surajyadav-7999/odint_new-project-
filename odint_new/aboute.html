About us

Odint Technology India Pvt Ltd was founded in the year 2017, with a goal in mind to become the most preferred wholesaler and trader of next generation Card Printers and Repairing Services.


  • we have established a strategic presence in the competitive industry by furnishing a board product portfolio of best in class software and hardware products, such as Card Printers, Card Printer Ribbon and ID Card Printer..

  • We provide a range of benefits in the form of hi-end products that are accepted by the end-users in the country for its superior quality, winning performance, ease of use, and a cost-effective advantage of integrated latest technology.
